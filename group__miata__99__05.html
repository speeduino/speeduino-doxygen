Detailed Description

Miata '99 to '05 with 4x 70 degree duration teeth running at cam speed. Teeth believed to be at the same angles as the 4g63 decoder. Tooth #1 is defined as the next crank tooth after the crank signal is HIGH when the cam signal is falling. Tooth number one is at 355* ATDC. (See: www.forum.diyefi.org/viewtopic.php?f=56&t=1077)
