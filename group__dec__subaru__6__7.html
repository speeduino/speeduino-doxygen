Detailed Description

Subaru 6/7 Trigger pattern decoder for 6 tooth (irregularly spaced) crank and 7 tooth (also fairly irregular) cam wheels (eg late 90's Impreza 2.2). This seems to be present in late 90's Subaru. In 2001 Subaru moved to 36-2-2-2 (See: http://www.vems.hu/wiki/index.php?page=InputTrigger%2FSubaruTrigger ).
