Detailed Description

Harley Davidson (V2) with 2 unevenly Spaced Teeth. Within the decoder code, the sync tooth is referred to as tooth #1. Derived from GMX7 and adapted for Harley. Only rising Edge is used for simplicity.The second input is ignored, as it does not help to resolve cam position.
